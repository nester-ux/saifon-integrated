Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 11, 2026

This Privacy Policy outlines how Supercat Multi-Point Clicker (hereinafter referred to as "SuperCat", "we", "our", or "us") handles your information when you use our Windows application. We are committed to ensuring your privacy and a secure user experience.

1. Information We Collect

S​uperCat is a local productivity tool. Unlike other services, we focus on privacy:

  • Personal Data:  We do not collect personal information such as names, contact details, or email addresses within the application.
  • Usage Data: We do not track your mouse movements, keyboard inputs, or session data for any external use. All automation tasks stay on your local machine.

2. How We Use Your Information

Since we do not collect personal data, we only use the local settings you provide to:

  • Execute automation tasks as per your configuration.
  • Improve the functionality and performance of the application on your device.

3. Legal Basis for Processing

We process data based on your consent and the necessity to provide the services you requested through the application.

4. Sharing Your Data

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data: No information is transmitted to third-party employers, clients, or servers. Your automation scripts and settings remain strictly on your local computer.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to ensure that the application operates safely within your Windows environment.

6. Data Retention

Any settings or configurations you create are stored locally and can be deleted by you at any time by clearing the app data or uninstalling the software.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to access the app's functions without fear of data tracking. If you have any concerns regarding how the app interacts with your system, please contact us.

8. Cookies

Our application does not use cookies or tracking pixels.
